Connect your bank (Stripe Connect)

Harve pays you through Stripe Connect. You’ll complete a short individual onboarding — identity and bank details — not a separate business entity.

Updated March 2026

Before you start

Have a government ID, your SSN last 4 (or regional equivalent), and bank details ready. Use the same legal name you use on your ID.

Steps in the app

  1. Open Harve and go to Settings (or the payouts section).
  2. Choose Connect bank or similar.
  3. We open Stripe’s hosted flow in your browser. Complete each step.
  4. Return to Harve — your status should show as connected when Stripe finishes review.

Individual vs business

Harve creates individual Connect accounts. If you see company or EIN fields, something may be wrong with your account — contact support@harve.ai so we can reset your payout profile.

Payout schedule

Payouts are scheduled on a weekly cadence (e.g. Mondays) once you’re active and above any minimum. Exact timing appears in the app.
